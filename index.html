¿Qué es el anime?

Anime refers to animated productions, usually originating from Japan, that feature colorful graphics, vibrant characters, and imaginative storytelling. Anime can be found in a wide variety of genres, including action, adventure, comedy, drama, fantasy, romance, science fiction, and more. Some anime are adaptations of manga (Japanese comics), while others are original works. Anime has become popular worldwide and has gained a large following, with fans of all ages and from various cultural backgrounds. In addition to traditional hand-drawn animation, modern anime also incorporates computer-generated imagery (CGI) and other advanced techniques to enhance the visual experience.
